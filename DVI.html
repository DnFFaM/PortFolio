DVI Monitor

Get comprehensive reports on your warehouse temperature, outside temperature and date & time. Use DVI to monitor (Warehouse and outside) temperature(°C/°F), date, time (London, Singapore, Copenhagen), stock status(Goods under min, goods over max, most sold goods today) and individual maps for every zone. Product Features - Measures indoor and outdoor temperature simultaneously. - Has a built - in screen that displays the day's weather forecast. - Records elapsed times from start to finish at midnight.
